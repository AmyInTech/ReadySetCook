What We Do

Recipes, Ingredients, & Instructions
to help with your busy schedule

Welcome to Ready Set Cook, an open-sourced database of recipes from around the world. For your convience, we provide recipes by ingredients, countries, and name.

Figuring out what to eat is a full time job. The entire process of making a meal come to life can be incredibly frustrating. That's why Ready Set Cook is ready to provide delicious ideas, recipes, ingredients, and instructions. Whether you are cooking for yourself or sharing a meal with your loved ones, we are happy to assist you in the journey of bringing a delicious and nutritious meal to the table with the most ease possible.
